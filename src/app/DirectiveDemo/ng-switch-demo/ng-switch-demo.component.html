NgSwitch DIRECTIVE The [ngSwitch] directive on a container specifies an expression to match against. The expressions to match are provided by ngSwitchCase directives on views within the container. Every view that matches is rendered. If there are no matches, a view with the ngSwitchDefault directive is rendered. Elements within the [NgSwitch] statement but outside of any NgSwitchCase or ngSwitchDefault directive are preserved at the location..
